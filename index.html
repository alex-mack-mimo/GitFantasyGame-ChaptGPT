Royal Castle 1

There was a rumor circulating in a small village about a hidden treasure in an old abandoned castle. The castle was said to have belonged to a wealthy noble family and was said to be filled with riches beyond imagination. The villagers were convinced that the treasure was still inside, waiting to be discovered. However, the truth was that the castle had been abandoned for many years and had fallen into disrepair. Many people had searched for the treasure, but none had found anything of value. The castle was a dangerous place to explore, with unstable walls and hidden pitfalls, and most people avoided it.
